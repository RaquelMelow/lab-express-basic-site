Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso, a Spanish racing driver, made his debut in Formula 1 with Minardi in 2001. Over his illustrious career, he achieved significant success with Renault, winning consecutive World Championships in 2005 and 2006. Alonso also competed for McLaren and Ferrari, coming close to additional titles. After a brief hiatus from Formula 1, he returned in 2021 with Alpine, showcasing his enduring competitive spirit despite not contending for the championship. Alonso's career is marked by his skill, determination, and notable contributions to the sport's history.
